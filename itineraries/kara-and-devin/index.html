Kara & Devin's Honeymoon

May 7-20, 2024

May 7th

Bienvenidos a España!

Arrival into MAD airport. Private transfer service from Hacienda Zorita will be there to pick you up and take you to the hotel for check in.

May 8th

11:00am: 90 minute massages at Hacienda Zorita's Wine & Olive Spa.

5:00pm: A Taste of Zorita: Enjoy the hotel's house made wine and craft rum with stories and education in their wine cellar.

May 9th

The ancient city of Salamanca welcomes you!

Hacienda Zorita's private transfer service has been arranged to take you to Salamanca.


May 10th

Check out from Hacienda Zorita. Their private transfer service will take you back to Salamanca for your bus ride at 12:30pm.


Bem-vindo a Porto!

5:30pm: Arrival into Porto via bus. There are often taxis around the terminal. Uber also is available and popular here.

Check in at Hotel Dom Henrique

Dinner: Open-ended depending on your hunger and energy levels after your bus trip. If you are looking for something immediate, the hotel's rooftop restaurant, 17°, is available. If you're up for exploring, well-rated restaurants within easy walking distance include:

  • MONTEEN
  • Bulha Sá Bandeira
  • Vaccarum
  • Aquele Tasco

May 11th

Porto exploration day!

Breakfast is offered at your hotel, but save some room for the many amazing opportunities for food you'll come across throughout the city!

3:30pm: 50min tour of Sandeman winery and cellar. Includes 3 tastings. Your ticket confirmations were sent to Devin's email.

Down the corner is Quinta do Noval if you're looking to continue the wine adventure. This is a casual shop where you can sit indoors or outside for varieties of tastings as well.
23 minute walk back over the bridge and through Porto (in the direction of your hotel) to Queijaria Amaral. This is an incredible hidden gem, widely known by locals as the best cheese shop and charcuterie cafe in the whole city. You can sit and get a cheese and meat board, some bread, more wine, and relax.
12 minute walk back to your hotel to relax until a late dinner.

8:30pm: Dinner reservation at Belos Aires Restaurante

May 11th

May 13th

Adeus Porto!

Before leaving the hotel, go check out Mercado do Bolhão, a sprawling bi-level market just a 5 minute walk away. Stock up on gifts and goodies for your road trip to the South!


Olá Lagos!

Your road trip comes to an end with your arrival at Vilabranca Turistic Apartments (AKA Agua Hotels Vila Branca).

7:30pm: Dinner reservations at Mar, a gorgeous seaside spot perfect for viewing the sunset. If the weather is mild, be sure to request an outdoor table upon arrival! From your hotel, this is either a 3 minute drive or a 15 minute walk.
