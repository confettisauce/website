11:00am to 12:30pm   Must arrive by 10:45am

3593 State Hwy 679

Your GPS might take you to the campground; continue past the campground towards the booth where you pay to enter the park. There's a turn-in BEFORE the booth where you should see the truck and trailer, which is the meeting point.

Important information for the Manatee and Dolphin Paddle

What to wear

  • Swimsuit/clothes that you don't mind getting a little wet
  • Shoes/sandals that can get wet

What to bring

  • Drinking water
  • Sunscreen
  • Sunglasses
  • Towel

Prohibited items

  • Metal containers
  • Glass
  • Any alcoholic beverages
  • Oil-based liquids
  • Tobacco products
  • Audio speakers

Theme of the night: Party Animals!

Wear your favorite animal print, and if you're feeling brave, ears, a tail, etc.

Early dinner at Fresco's Waterfront Bistro

Fresco's is a no-reservation/no-call-ahead restaurant. They will get busy later in the evening and your sunset boat tour is scheduled for 7:00pm. Therefore, I would suggest arriving at Fresco's between 4:45 and 5:00pm

Important information for the Sunset Boat Ride

$100 in cash due upon charter liftoff. Captain's charge an additional $50 per hour for groups of more than 6 passengers. This separate fee will go directly to your captain on the day of the charter in cash.

Although not required, captains work for greatly appreciated tips. Totally Tiki Tours encourages you to tip 20% of the charter's cost.
